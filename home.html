Key Details

The ride starts and ends in NYC with three distance options: 30, 60, or 100 miles. We cycle to celebrate the world's most historic LGBT Pride Celebration, presented by K-Y. The 5th annual NYC Pride Ride is a day of inspiration, commemoration and celebration. Join us on Sunday, June 11th.

What To Expect:

A fun and exhilaration day of cycling on the roads of NY and NJ.

The fully supported ride includes:

  • Detailed cue sheets and marked roads
  • Four fully supported rest stops
  • A fun and food-filled festival with barbecue at the end of the ride
  • Free massages at the Finish Festival thanks to our friends and long-time sponsor, MMX Studios.
  • and 2017 NYC Pride Ride T-Shirt!

    • Help The Community As You Ride

    OutCycling is committed to promoting healthy living through cycling to the LGBT community in NYC and beyond. A portion of ride proceeds will go to the development of OutCycling's Youth Cycling Program, The Fearless Flyers, a program designed to educate and encourage LGBT youth to make healthy lifestyle choices.
